Absolutely. We use 100% solids epoxy systems and urethane concrete specifically designed for heavy forklift traffic, chemical spills, and extreme conditions. Most floors last 15-20+ years with proper maintenance.

Do you work around our production schedule?

Yes‚Äîthat's our specialty. We've done weekend installs, after-hours work, and phased sections to keep production running. Laura coordinates everything to minimize disruption.

What's included in the free quote & site assessment?

We'll visit your facility, measure square footage, assess current floor condition, test for moisture issues, and provide a detailed quote with material options and timeline. No obligation.

Yes. We're certified installers for Sherwin Williams, Dur-a-Flex, Elite Crete, and Prosoco. Fully licensed and insured in PA, NJ, and DE.
