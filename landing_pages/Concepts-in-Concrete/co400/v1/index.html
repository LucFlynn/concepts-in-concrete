Common Questions About Industrial Flooring

How long does installation take?

Most industrial projects complete in 2-3 days. Larger facilities (10,000+ sq ft) may take 4-5 days. We can work nights and weekends to avoid disrupting your operations.

When can we resume operations?

Light foot traffic after 24 hours, heavy equipment after 72 hours. Full chemical resistance achieved after 7 days. We provide a detailed schedule for your specific needs.

What's included in the 5-year warranty?

Coverage includes delamination, excessive wear, and material defects. We also offer optional annual maintenance packages to extend floor life beyond 10 years.

Can you work with our existing floor?

Yes, we can resurface most concrete floors. Our team will assess during the free evaluation and recommend the best approach for your situation.

Do you handle floor repairs first?

Absolutely. We repair cracks, level uneven areas, and address moisture issues before applying the epoxy system. This ensures maximum adhesion and longevity.
